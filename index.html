Everything you need to know about Lexa

What is Lexa?

Lexa is an AI-powered second brain that helps you organize, search, and chat with your documents. Upload your notes, PDFs, and ideas ‚Äî we'll make them searchable and organized.

What is the PARA method?

PARA stands for Projects, Areas, Resources, and Archives. It's a proven organization system by Tiago Forte. Lexa automatically categorizes your documents into these four categories.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your documents are encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your data or use it to train AI models. Your knowledge stays yours.

What file types are supported?

PDF, TXT, Markdown, DOC, DOCX, CSV, JSON, HTML, XML, RTF, and LOG files. More formats coming soon.

How does AI chat work?

The AI reads your documents and can answer questions about them. It uses only your uploaded content to provide relevant answers. Available on Pro tier and above.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
